This is Elnaz Doostdar

I’m specialized in product and UX/UI design and believe in the power of research, experiment with different approaches, failing fast, and constantly reflecting on past experiences for improvement.

Problem Solver

I’m naturally a problem solver owing to my background in studying maths at college and engineering at university. I realized user-centered design aligns perfectly with this skill and started to pursue it professionally by completing a user-centered design certificate back in 2015. The fact that it touches other areas of science like psychology, aesthetic, research and more importantly impacts people's lives directly makes it so interesting and dynamic.

No matter what project I’m working on whether it’s designing a new feature for a product, or enhancing the existing experiences, I always look for the most efficient way of delivering solutions based on the mindset of the people I’m designing for. That makes me not stick to a certain process all the time and take certain approaches based on the context and the problem I’m trying to solve.

Collaborator

I love the collaborative work environment. As the saying goes “Two heads are better than one”. I really enjoy sharing my ideas with different roles in the team and try to include them from the start in the design process. They help me expand my perspective and receive impactful feedback to guide my design.

Additionally, I believe that different people have different motivations for a project and articulating solutions effectively is important based on what matters to them. My background in coding comes handy to communicate specs with developers because I know how things work in the real world.

Tech Fan

I’m a big technology fan and excited about the upcoming changes to the digital world. The capabilities that seemed impossible in the past have become available not only visually but virtually and through voice. These new aspects have broadened the spectrum of design and created a new field to explore design opportunities. We as designers are not limited to a certain platform anymore yet we can still apply the same principles to come up with great solutions.

Technologies such as augmented reality can be utilized to create memorable user experiences that can be effectively used in the field of healthcare, marketing, especially education because it makes learning things easier and more durable.

As a technology enthusiast I’m excited to constantly explore and learn these platforms and the new opportunities they bring to the field of design.
