This is Elnaz Doostdar

I’ve been designing enterprise solutions and MVP features for over 6 years.

From when I was little I was obsessed with solving puzzles and continued to have this passion since then. My curiosity took me through an exciting academic journey, from BA in computer hardware engineering, to MA in web development and finally a Cert in user experience and customer-centered design to become a UX designer. I love to take initiatives and explore different areas of science to expand my perspective in life.

For the last few years, I've been persistent to learn and apply UX best practices to the elegant interfaces I designed across various platforms. My goal is to make a positive impact on how the technology works through designing enduring experiences for people.

Besides design, you can find me working on my side projects, hiking, or trying different recipes that allow me to be creative in different ways.
